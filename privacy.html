Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At SEREN, your privacy is not just a feature—it's a fundamental principle. We've designed this app to keep your personal information where it belongs: with you.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Mood and Wellness Data: Your mood check-ins, journal entries, and exercise usage
  • Personalization Settings: Your preferences, goals, and customized exercise routines
  • Usage Statistics: How you use the app (stored locally for your benefit only)

Information We May Collect

  • Subscription Status: Managed through Apple's App Store (we only verify your subscription status)
  • Anonymous Analytics: If you opt-in, we may collect anonymized usage data to improve the app (no personal identifiers)

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your name or email (unless you choose to contact support)
  • ❌ Your location data
  • ❌ Your journal entries or mood logs (these never leave your device)
  • ❌ Your contacts, photos, or other personal data
  • ❌ Advertising identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Local Data: Used solely to provide you with personalized experiences within the app
  • Subscription Data: Used only to verify your access to premium features
  • Analytics (if opted-in): Used to improve app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All wellness data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms
  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest on your device
  • No Cloud Sync (MVP): Your personal data does not sync to our servers
  • Your Control: You can delete all app data at any time through iOS Settings

4. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Period.

Third-Party Services We Use:

  • Apple App Store: For subscription management (subject to Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat/Superwall: For subscription infrastructure (only processes subscription status, not personal wellness data)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data (it's on your device)
  • Delete all your data (through app settings or by deleting the app)
  • Opt-out of analytics (through app settings)
  • Request information about how your data is used (contact us anytime)

6. Children's Privacy

SEREN is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

7. International Users

If you use SEREN outside the United States, please note that your data remains on your device. We comply with GDPR and other international privacy regulations.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app. Your continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Data Retention

  • While App is Installed: Data remains on your device as long as the app is installed
  • After Uninstall: All local data is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Subscription Data: Retained by Apple per their policies

11. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

  • Consent: For optional analytics
  • Contract Performance: For subscription management
  • Legitimate Interest: For app functionality and improvement
