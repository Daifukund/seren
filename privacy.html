Before using the Service or submitting any personal data to SEREN., please review this Privacy Policy carefully and contact us if you have any questions. 1. What Data We Collect

We collect information that alone or in combination with other information in our possession could be used to identify you ("Personal Data") as follows:

Personal Data You Provide

We collect the following categories of Personal Data from you when you use the Service or communicate with us:

Identification Data: We collect your email address (if provided voluntarily), name or nickname you may provide, and unique anonymous identifiers assigned by third-party service providers (such as analytics or subscription management tools). No mandatory account creation is required to use SEREN.

Anxiety and Mood Tracking Data: The anxiety check-in and mood tracking features help you measure your emotional state. All of the individual-level data you provide in connection with this feature is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. We may collect anonymized, aggregated mood trend data (e.g., number of users who reported feeling better after using a technique) through our analytics provider for the purpose of improving our service.

Usage Statistics: We collect anonymized information relating to your app usage, such as which features you use (breathing exercises, grounding techniques, SOS flow), session duration, and interaction patterns. This data is collected through our analytics provider Mixpanel to help us understand how users benefit from SEREN and improve the app experience.

Subscription Data: When you purchase a subscription through the App, we use RevenueCat to manage your subscription status and entitlements. RevenueCat assigns you an anonymous customer ID that is not linked to your personal identity. We do not collect or process credit or debit card data - all payment processing is handled securely by Apple or Google through their respective app stores.

Communication Data: We may collect information when you contact us with questions or concerns through our support email. Providing this information is voluntary.

Internet Activity Data

When you use the App, we automatically collect certain technical information:

Device Data: Device type, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers. This information is collected anonymously through Mixpanel.

Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app, such as which features you use, how long you spend in the app, and which techniques you complete. This data is anonymized and used solely to improve the app experience and understand what helps our users most effectively manage anxiety.

Technical Data: App crashes, performance metrics, and error logs to help us identify and fix technical issues.

Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party service providers:

Mixpanel (Analytics): We use Mixpanel to collect anonymous analytics about how users interact with SEREN. This helps us understand which features are most helpful and where we can improve. Mixpanel may collect device identifiers and usage patterns, but this data is not used for advertising or shared with advertising networks. Learn more at Mixpanel's Privacy Policy.

RevenueCat (Subscription Management): We use RevenueCat to manage in-app subscriptions and verify subscription status. RevenueCat assigns anonymous customer IDs and does not collect personally identifiable information. Learn more at RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Apple/Google (Payment Processing): All subscription payments are processed through Apple's App Store or Google Play Store. We never have access to your payment card information. Apple and Google may provide us with limited anonymized transaction data (such as subscription type and status) necessary to provide you with the Service.

2. How We Use Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Service: Enable core functionality like breathing exercises, mood tracking, and anxiety relief techniques
  • To understand user behavior: Analyze anonymized usage patterns to improve features and develop new tools that better serve our users
  • To manage subscriptions: Verify subscription status and provide access to premium features
  • To provide customer support: Respond to your inquiries and technical support requests
  • To maintain app quality: Identify and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues
  • To comply with legal obligations: Respond to legal requests and prevent fraud or misuse of the Service

Aggregated and Anonymized Data: We may use aggregated, anonymized data that does not identify individual users to understand general usage trends, improve our Service, and share insights about mental health app usage with the broader research community (e.g., "80% of users reported feeling calmer after using box breathing").

What We DON'T Do:

  • ❌ We do NOT use your data for advertising or marketing to you
  • ❌ We do NOT sell your personal data
  • ❌ We do NOT share your mood or anxiety data with third parties
  • ❌ We do NOT track you across other apps or websites
  • ❌ We do NOT use your health data for purposes unrelated to providing you the Service
  • ❌ We do NOT work with advertising networks or data brokers

3. How We Share Data

SEREN. does not sell your Personal Data. We only share data in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers: We share data with Mixpanel (analytics) and RevenueCat (subscription management) as described above, solely to provide and improve the Service. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Legal Compliance: We may disclose data if required by law, legal process, or government request, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

Business Transfers: If SEREN. is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

With Your Consent: We may share data for other purposes with your explicit consent.

Important: Your mood tracking and anxiety check-in data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers or shared with anyone.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy:

  • Mood and anxiety tracking data: Stored locally on your device only. You can delete this data at any time by deleting the app.
  • Analytics data: Retained by Mixpanel in anonymized form for up to 5 years for trend analysis.
  • Subscription data: Retained by RevenueCat for the duration of your subscription plus up to 1 year for accounting and support purposes.
  • Support communications: Retained for up to 2 years to help us improve our support and address recurring issues.

Push Notifications: You can enable or disable push notifications in your device settings at any time.

9. Apple HealthKit Integration

If you choose to enable Apple HealthKit integration (optional feature), SEREN. may write mindfulness minutes and breathing session data to your Apple Health app. This data:

  • Is stored locally on your device in the Apple Health app
  • Is never transmitted to SEREN.'s servers
  • Is never shared with third parties or used for marketing
  • Can be deleted at any time through the Apple Health app

You can revoke SEREN.'s access to HealthKit data at any time in your device settings: Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → SEREN.
